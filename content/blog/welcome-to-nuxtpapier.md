# Welcome to NuxtPapier

Welcome to my new blog! This is NuxtPapier, a minimal blog built with Nuxt 3 and inspired by the clean, distraction-free design of Bear Blog.

## Why Another Blog?

In a world full of noise, social media distractions, and cluttered websites, sometimes you just want to focus on what matters: **the words**. That's exactly what Bear Blog represents, and that's what I wanted to recreate with modern web technologies.

## The Philosophy

This blog follows a simple philosophy:

- **Content first** - No unnecessary widgets or social media integrations
- **Fast loading** - Minimal CSS and JavaScript
- **Clean typography** - Easy to read, focused on the text
- **Distraction-free** - No ads, no popups, no noise

## Built With

- **Nuxt 3** - The modern Vue.js framework
- **Nuxt Content** - File-based CMS for markdown content
- **Minimal CSS** - Just enough styling to be readable and pleasant

## What's Next?

I plan to write about web development, minimalism in design, and thoughts on technology. Stay tuned for more posts!

Thanks for reading, and welcome to NuxtPapier.
